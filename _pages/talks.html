Music AI in Concerts

Automatic Accompaniment with a Real Violinist

I performed Salut d'Amour with Violinist Leia Zhu during her visit at MBZUAI in Feb, 2024. It was my first time to test a computer music algorithm with a true musician on a concert!

Music AI Concert in Dubai Presented by Music X Lab

In summer 2022, I hosted a music AI concert with other lab members. We demonstrated some of our music education and generation algorithms with some music.

Deep Music Generation for Human-Machine Co-Creation

Style Transfer from Audio and Keyboard Input

I visited Teo, the music robot, in Nov, 2023. I invited him to arrange Fly Me to the Moon in different styles I showezd to him.

Whole-Song Accompaniment Arrangement

Then, I invited Teo, the music robot, to accompany Gus's rendition of Remember Me, while I used a keyboard to cue the tempo, acting as a conductor.

Steering the Sound: an Education Software Prototype

If one feels hard to play 88 keys on a piano, use a steering wheel powered by our music representation learning model. The interface is designed by Will Huang.

A Chorus Arranged by AI

In 2022, our lab was invited to compose a four-part chorus with a piano accompaniment with our music generation algorithms.
